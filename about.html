Our Story

Founded in 1995, Shobha Medical Stores has been serving the community for over 25 years with a commitment to providing quality healthcare products and exceptional service.

What started as a small neighborhood pharmacy has grown into a trusted name in healthcare, serving thousands of families with their medical and wellness needs.

We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of quality, authenticity, and customer care in everything we do.
